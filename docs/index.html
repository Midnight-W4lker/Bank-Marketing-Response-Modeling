Problem

What the analysis needed to answer

Prioritize customers for a bank marketing campaign while separating realistic pre-call targeting signals from post-call information.

A campaign-response pipeline audits segments, compares with-duration and pre-call models, tunes regularized logistic regression, and uses threshold planning for contact strategy.

Findings

The signals that mattered most

  • Response is highly imbalanced, so accuracy alone is misleading.
  • Duration is powerful but only known after a call, so it is not suitable for pre-call targeting.
  • Prior outcome, contact channel, month, age, balance, and loan indicators are more realistic targeting signals.
  • Thresholds should reflect campaign capacity, contact cost, and customer fatigue risk.
Coverage

What the notebook covers

  • Unknown-value and response-balance audit
  • Segment analysis by job, education, loan status, month, and prior outcome
  • Statistical tests, correlation, and VIF review
  • With-duration versus pre-call model comparison
  • Regularized logistic regression, coefficients, ROC, precision-recall, and threshold planning
